studio practice

I started thinking about the apocalypse museum concept in spring 2021, in the second spring time of the coronavirus. Working through a junior studio class in the privacy/isolation/luxury/misery of my shared apartment, the concept of ends and beginnings was hard to escape. In my mind the apocalypse museum is both a museum from some far off future documenting a yet-unrealized end times and also a museum that has weathered the end times and stands as a ruined monument / persevering ruins.
